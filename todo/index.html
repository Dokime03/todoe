Turn any text into a task with ease for yourself
or your coworker

We chat with any co workers, like any of you.

We chat Work with them mostly,like any of you.

And we chat a lot of work on facebook chat itself.



In these chats there is exchange of many thoughts . Now you can mark these actionable thoughts as to items into your or your coworkers to do lists right
from within those chats.

Now use this fastest todo list as the first point of reference for organising your
tasks on task management tools like asana, producteev, todoist etc. If you
use these tools. However, we notice that for some of us todotodo
is more than enough to keep us on top of our tasks.

We are on a mission to boost productivity by enabling actionable thoughts to be shared with fellow co workers in the fastest way.
